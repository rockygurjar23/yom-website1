About YOM

YOM is a visionary agroforestry initiative focused on transforming degraded land into vibrant, sustainable forests that provide environmental and economic benefits.

Our Vision

To expand green cover, restore soil health, and empower rural economies through nature-positive agroforestry practices.

Mission

We aim to plant diverse, climate-resilient crops and trees, improving biodiversity, capturing carbon, and creating long-term livelihoods.
